# Gemini-Powered-Restaurant-Automator

📋 Business Requirements Document (BRD)
Focus: The "Why" and the high-level business goals.

1. Project Overview
To provide a 24/7 automated assistant for restaurants that reduces the need for human staff to take basic orders and answer repetitive questions.

2. Objectives

Customer Accessibility: Allow customers to order via WhatsApp (the most used messaging app).

Efficiency: Automate the hand-off between a customer's request and the kitchen's order list.

Accuracy: Real-time inventory tracking to prevent over-ordering of out-of-stock items.

3. Key Stakeholders

Restaurant Owners (for reporting/oversight).

Kitchen Staff (as recipients of the orders).

End Customers (for a frictionless ordering experience).

4. High-Level Requirements

The system must understand natural language (e.g., "I want 2 burgers but no onions").

The system must check stock levels before confirming an order.
